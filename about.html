About Us

Developer Student Club GEC, Rajkot is inspired by Google Developers Family. We started our journey in Feb 2019. We are a group of passionate students who try to engage fellow student developers through our events, workshops, sessions and meetups. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & developers in and around the Campus.
